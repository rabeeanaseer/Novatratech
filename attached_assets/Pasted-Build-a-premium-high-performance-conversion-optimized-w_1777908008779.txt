## ⚙️ TECH STACK REQUIREMENTS

* Use **Next.js (App Router)**
* Use **Tailwind CSS**
* Use **Framer Motion for animations**
* Fully responsive (mobile-first)
* Optimized performance (Lighthouse 90+)
* Clean modular components
* SEO optimized structure

---

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

Style:

* Dark modern UI with premium feel
* Inspired by Stripe, Vercel, Linear, and top SaaS companies

Colors:

* Background: #0B0F19 (deep dark)
* Primary: #5B8CFF (electric blue)
* Accent: #00FFC6 (neon cyan)
* Text: #E6EAF2 (light grey)
* Secondary text: #9AA4B2

Typography:

* Headings: bold, large, tight spacing
* Body: clean, readable
* Use modern sans-serif (Inter or similar)

* Smooth animations (no lag)
* Subtle gradients and glow effects
